Hello,
My name is Aman and I am a data scientist. In this video, I explain how different subject areas of business can be helped using data science and machine learning. I take the example of customer analytics and expain five different use cases from customer analytics. I explain below use cases at high level.
1. Customer segmentation
2. Customer attrition models
3. Customer next best offer model
4. Customer RFM model/score
5. Customer CLTV model

The videos uploaded will not be very technical in nature and hence it can be easily grasped by viewers from different background as well.

Publicado em 26 de mai. de 2020

There are hundreds of data science courses in the market today and selecting the right one can be intimidating! During this one-hour webinar, Joe San Pietro will continue with our “Myths and Facts" series where we will explore the most important points to consider when selecting a data science program.

Besides the sheer number of data science courses out there, it seems like there are an equal number of instruction formats offered such as: online, on-campus, instructor-led, self-paced, etc. Furthermore, each program says that they’re the best and can train you to be a data scientist in just a few months. It is a lot to take in for sure! The goal of this webinar is to help make the decision process easier and more clear as we separate the myths from the facts.

Key Takeaways:
What are the most important skills that I need to learn in a data science program?
What is the best learning format that will help me reach my career goals while balancing my busy life?
Is an immersive certificate program right for me or should I break up my learning journey over the longer term?

In this episode, Sanyam Bhutani interviews Emmanuel Ameisen, ML engineer at Stripe, and the author of the O'Reilly book: "Building Machine Learning Powered Applications: Going from idea to product"

In this interview they talk about Emmanuel's journey into machine learning, and how his journey through the different roles led him to writing the book.

The book is one of the greatest resources for the title for Building ML Apps, and also one of the greatest top down learning books that follow the top down teaching approach.

They talk about who the book is a really for, what can you expect out of it and Emanuel's journey of writing to Book and where to go after you've read the book, How do you find your idea to take to project your passion project or your million dollar app idea.

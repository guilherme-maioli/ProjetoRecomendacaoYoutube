Purpose of Data science-

The main purpose of data science is to find patterns in the data. It uses various statistical techniques to analyze the data and obtain information from them. After data extraction, litigation, and preprocessing, a data scientist must review the data completely. It is therefore responsible for making predictions from the data. The goal of a data scientist is to conclude the data. These results can help companies make smarter business decisions. We will divide this blog into different sections to better understand the role of a data scientist.


Why data is important

The data is the new flow. We live in the era of the fourth industrial revolution. It is the era of artificial intelligence and big data. There is a huge data explosion that has resulted in new technologies and smarter products. Data needs have grown enormously over the past decade. Many companies have focused on data. Data has created new industries in the IT industry.
Data science is a whole new terminology. Before data science, we had statisticians. These statisticians had experience in analyzing qualitative data and companies used it to analyze their performance and overall sales. With the advent of an IT process, storage, and analysis tools in the cloud, the IT field has merged with statistics. This created data science.

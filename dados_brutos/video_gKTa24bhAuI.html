Join NASA, the European Space Agency (ESA), the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA), the Canadian Space Agency (CSA), and the National Centre for Space Studies (CNES) for the Space Apps COVID-19 Challenge virtual hackathon on May 30-31!
